about us

The TOGIVE site is actually a platform where you can find voluntary events according to a personal search and take a voluntary part in them. On this site, people who are interested in giving and contributing to the community will be able to find specific volunteer events of associations that would be happy to receive the help of those potential volunteers. Today there are many people who want to contribute to others and volunteer in associations but cannot remain committed to the same association for a long time, since in order to participate in association events it is often necessary to take an active and prolonged part. TOGIVE provides a solution to the problem and presents users with specific events in which they can take part without committing to volunteer regularly. This way, people who find themselves without plans at certain hours on a specific day will be able to use this time to give to society and feel significant.
